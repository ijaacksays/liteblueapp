Step-by-Step Guide to Updating Personal Information on LiteBlue

1. Log into Your LiteBlue Account

To begin updating your personal information:

  • Go to the LiteBlue website.
  • Enter your Employee ID and USPS PIN.
  • Click on the Log In button.

2. Navigate to Personal Information Section

Once logged in:

  • Click on the My HR tab at the top of the page.
  • Select Life Changes from the dropdown menu. This section allows you to manage various personal details.

3. Update Your Address

To change your address:

  • Click on Change My Address.
  • You will see your current address displayed.
  • Click the Edit button next to your address.
  • Enter your new address details in the provided fields.
  • After entering your new address, click Review to check for accuracy.

4. Update Your Phone Number and Emergency Contacts

Updating your phone number and emergency contacts is equally straightforward:

  • Under the Life Changes section, select Change Phone/Emergency Contacts.
  • Click Edit next to the current phone number or emergency contact.
  • Input your new details and click Review, then Save once confirmed.

5. Confirm Changes

After making all necessary updates:

  • Ensure that all changes are correct by reviewing them one last time.
  • Click on the Save button to finalize updates.

6. Log Out Safely

Once you have saved all changes:

  • Close your browser window to log out securely from LiteBlue.
  • When you log back in later, you should see your updated information reflected in your profile.
